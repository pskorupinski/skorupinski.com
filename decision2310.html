Organization
Context of hiringT-Systems - German IT consulting corporate - “bets of car industry growth”. They mentioned they want to grow their business twice by 2026. They are hiring between Germany (leading the projects), Spain and India, and developing an international “car centre of competency”.In 2022, Codurance - a “quality software delivery” consulting mid-sized UK-Spanish company, opened a new service line focused on product development. Meanwhile, they hired the first few product managers. One of the co-founders is leading efforts to further shape the positioning of this service line.
Position to succeedThey mentioned the objective is to offer OEM services in the area of connected cars to all their clients (e.g., VW, Mercedes, BMW), as everyone wants to “catch up with Tesla”.They developed a solid portfolio of clients across industries since founded 10 years ago. They discovered this positioning of theirs that they are “the best at the mix of all skills” to deliver a software product.
Role
Context of the positionIn November, they start onboarding for a new OEM project. Also, they may want to involve PMs to go and do a product management work on the service line itself, exploring the market needs and refining positioning.
AccountabilityI will most likely be accountable for a part of delivery, from refining clear requirements with stakeholders and developers to coordinating release activities with other teams.I will sure be expected to do high-quality consulting, which requires strong communication and management skills to set expectations, influence and navigate the team through the delivery.
Decision-making authorityAbsolutely unknown, however, I am not expecting here anything more than a communication and operational role.I will most likely have a decent autonomy in the projects I am involved in, naturally, to the extent that the particular client will want to work.
Internal fit

Opportunity to leverage my strengths and knowledgeIt is building on my knowledge, however, it is mostly a new thing that I will need to adapt to.I feel like here, I will have a chance to leverage one way or another almost everything I ever learned.
